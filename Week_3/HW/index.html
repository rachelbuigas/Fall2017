Mango Salsa

I bet this mango salsa would be fantastic on fish tacos or just about any fresh, Mexican-themed dish, really. I can confirm that it goes well with chips, guacamole and black bean tacos. You could also serve it on salad or just eat it straight out of the bowl as a salad. It’s that good. (I stole this from a recipe on cookieandkatie.com).

Ingredients

  • 1 mango, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup peeled, diced cucumber
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped jalapeno
  • 1/3 cup diced red onion
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/3 cup roughly chopped cilantro leaves
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Combine the mango, cucumber, jalapeno, red onion, lime juice and cilantro leaves and mix well.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.

Details

Time: 5 min
Yeild: 4 servings

Nutrition per serving:
Calories: 44
Fat: 0.2 g
Carbohydrates: 11 g
Fiber: 1 g
Protien: 0.6 g
